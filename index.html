About Birch & Bloom Counselling

Birch & Bloom Counselling is committed to helping clients navigate life’s challenges and achieve meaningful personal growth in a safe, supportive, and confidential space.

We use a range of therapeutic approaches—including cognitive-behavioural therapy, solution-focused therapy, and mindfulness-based techniques—to tailor each session to your unique needs.

Our goal is to address mental health challenges, enhance well-being, and promote lasting change.

Services include individual and family counselling, with flexible session lengths of 60, 90, or 120 minutes to fit your schedule.

With a client-centred, trauma-informed approach, we ensure every client feels heard, supported, and empowered on their therapeutic journey.

FAQs

What is a consultation, and do I need one before starting therapy?
A consultation is a brief, no‐obligation meeting to determine if therapy is the right fit.
What is the process for starting therapy?
The first step is to book a 15-minute consultation. This is an opportunity to discuss your needs and goals.
How do I prepare for my first session?
Before your first session, it’s helpful to think about your goals and any questions you have.
Is therapy confidential?
Yes, therapy is confidential with exceptions only for safety or legal obligations.
How does billing and insurance coverage work?
Direct billing is available for most major insurance providers. You can also submit receipts for reimbursement.
How do I book a session, and what is your booking policy?
You can book online or via email. We ask for 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling.
How do I know if my therapist is the right fit for me?
During your initial consultation, we discuss your preferences and concerns to ensure a good match.
