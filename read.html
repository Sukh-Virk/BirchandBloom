Ontario Crisis & Mental Health Support Resources

Feb 25 Written by Arandeep Virk

If you or someone you know is in crisis, help is available 24/7. Please reach out to one of the following resources.

Immediate Crisis Support

  • 9-8-8 Suicide Crisis Helpline
    Call or text 9-8-8 for free, 24/7 support across Canada.
    More info: 988.ca
  • 9-1-1
    For life-threatening emergencies.

Toronto

  • Toronto Distress Centres
    24/7 support for individuals in crisis.
    Call: 416-408-4357 (HELP)
    More info: www.dcogt.com
  • Gerstein Crisis Centre
    Provides crisis intervention services.
    Call: 416-929-5200
    More info: gersteincentre.org
  • Toronto Rape Crisis Centre / Multicultural Women Against Rape
    24-hour crisis line for survivors of sexual violence.
    Call: 416-597-8808
    More info: trccmwar.ca

Brampton

  • Spectra Helpline
    Offers 24/7 multilingual distress lines.
    Call: 905-459-7777 TTY: 905-278-4890
    Languages: English, Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu, Spanish, Portuguese
    More info: www.spectrahelpline.org
  • Peel Crisis Services
    Provides immediate crisis intervention.
    Call: 905-278-9036
    More info: cmhapeeldufferin.ca

London

  • Reach Out
    24/7 information, support, and crisis services for mental health and addictions.
    Call: 519-433-2023 or 1-866-933-2023
    More info: reachout247.ca
  • Support Line (CMHA Thames Valley)
    Provides confidential listening and support.
    Call: 519-601-8055 or 1-844-360-8055
    More info: cmhatv.ca

Additional Resources

  • Assaulted Women's Helpline
    24/7 crisis line for women experiencing abuse.
    Call: 1-866-863-0511 TTY: 1-866-863-7868
    More info: www.awhl.org
  • Kids Help Phone
    24/7 support for youth across Canada.
    Call: 1-800-668-6868 Text CONNECT to 686868
    More info: kidshelpphone.ca
  • ConnexOntario
    Free, confidential health-service referrals for alcohol, drugs, mental health, gambling.
    Call: 1-866-531-2600
    More info: www.connexontario.ca
  • 211 Ontario
    Info & referrals for community, government, social & health services across Ontario.
    Call: 2-1-1
    More info: 211ontario.ca
